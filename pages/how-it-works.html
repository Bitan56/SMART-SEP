HOW IT WORKS

General Implementation

Urban Integration

Reduces collection costs by 40% through optimized routing based on real-time bin capacity data.

1. Detection

Ultrasonic sensors detect the arrival of waste at the bin entrance, triggering the system to wake up from standby mode.

2. AI Classification

The ESP32-CAM captures an image. Our neural network classifies the waste as Biodegradable or Non-Biodegradable in milliseconds.

3. Smart Segregation

The high-torque robotic arm receives the classification signal and physically moves the waste into the correct designated bin.

4. Cloud Sync

Fill levels, gas concentrations, and system health are uploaded to our real-time cloud database for remote monitoring.
